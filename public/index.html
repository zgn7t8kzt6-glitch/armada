Owner's Guide

Every feature and exactly where to find it. Staff see only their own tools — this is your full map.

🕐 My Shift — top of the app, everyone

  • Clock in / out — first thing; puts you on shift (locked to the building or office WiFi).
  • Facility at a glance — patients here, scheduled, admitted, discharged today.
  • Who's on shift + the Lead — the crew, with a 📞 Call-the-lead button (defaults to Brandon).
  • Last shift's report — read it before you start; write yours for the next shift.
  • Alerts — critical only (clear each shift), Needs you now ranked to-dos, Shift checklist, My Tasks, and My Role at the bottom.

👥 Team — leadership

  • Employees — every person: tap for their profile → performance %, Big Five + DISC personality, "How Horst would lead them" (AI), coaching log, what makes them tick. Leadership-only.
  • How I'm doing — each person's own scorecard (and your team summary).
  • Hiring, Training, Library / SOPs, Messages.

🛏️ Stay — daily care

  • Client Voice — all kiosk feedback in one place (reach-outs, suggestions, surveys, meal comments) with a date filter + new badge.
  • Rounds / Round Status — scan each room, tap each client's condition.
  • Bed Turnover — auto-flags a bed when someone's discharged; clean → done; manager-only dismiss.
  • Laundry, Meals (snack station, timeliness, ratings), Belongings (the cash/valuables controls), Incidents (behavioral contracts).

🚪 Arrival & Intake

  • Intake checklist — greet, search belongings, dignity bag, scrubs, Care Card. The Belongings form is required — it can't be checked off by hand.
  • Front Desk / Arrivals, Admissions, Referrals & Partners.

🔒 The money controls — Stay → Belongings

  • Cash in & out: second-staff witness + client's printed name & signature + optional photo of the cash.
  • Sealed bag #, storage location, surprise audits (flag discrepancies), big cash-out alerts, and a discharge guard so no one leaves with belongings unreturned.

⚙️ Command — admin

  • Command Center — census, flow, alert-response scorecard.
  • Settings → Automation — performance targets (rounds/snack per shift), reminders.
  • Settings → Clock-in location lock — set the geofence + Allow this WiFi.
  • Users — add staff (email invite to sign up), set roles & phone numbers. Revenue, Budget, Documentation, 90-Day Plan.
