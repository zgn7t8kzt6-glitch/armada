How this works

This tool follows the Ritz-Carlton service model, adapted for recovery care:

  • Guest Preference Pad → Client Care Card. You capture each client's preferences, goals, triggers, and the personal touches that make them feel truly cared for — once.
  • Daily Lineup → Shift Playbook. The app turns those cards into a precise, role-specific checklist of what each staff member should do for each client, this shift.
  • Anticipatory service. Safety watch-items and personal touches surface automatically so nothing falls through the cracks across shift changes.

Data is stored locally in this browser only (nothing is sent to a server). Use Export to back up or move data between devices. For real client/PHI use, see the deployment notes your team received.
